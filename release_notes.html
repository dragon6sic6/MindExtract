What's New in 1.5.2

  • Fixed garbled transcription text — Removed WhisperKit special tokens (<|5.92|>, <|startoftranscript|>, etc.) from transcription output
  • Download progress in transcription window — When transcribing from a URL, the transcription window now shows download progress with percentage
  • Context-aware waiting animation — Shows what's happening: downloading audio, loading model, extracting audio, or transcribing
  • Improved transcription history — History now stores segment data (timestamps, confidence) so reopened transcriptions have full timeline view
